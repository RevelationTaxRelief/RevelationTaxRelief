Our Professional Tax Resolution Services

Comprehensive & Ethical Tax Relief

Revelation Tax Relief offers a range of professional services designed to address various tax challenges with integrity and expertise. We provide structured guidance and representation, focusing on compliant and sustainable resolutions. Our services are tailored to your specific situation, ensuring a personalized and ethical approach.

Important Disclaimer: Pricing for our services varies significantly based on the complexity and scope of each individual case. Furthermore, Revelation Tax Relief does not guarantee specific outcomes or results, as all final determinations and resolutions are made solely by the relevant tax authorities (e.g., IRS, state tax agencies).

IRS Audit Representation

Navigating an IRS audit can be intimidating. We provide professional representation, ensuring your rights are protected and all documentation is accurately presented. Our goal is to facilitate a smooth process and achieve a fair resolution.

Back Tax Resolution

If you owe back taxes, we can help explore options such as Installment Agreements, Offers in Compromise, or Currently Not Collectible status. We work to find a manageable solution that aligns with your financial circumstances.

Tax Liens and Levies

Facing a tax lien or levy requires immediate action. We assist in understanding these enforcement actions and work diligently to negotiate their release or modification, protecting your assets and financial stability.

Penalty Abatement

Tax penalties can significantly increase your tax burden. We evaluate your eligibility for penalty abatement based on reasonable cause or administrative relief, and prepare the necessary requests for submission to tax authorities.

Wage Garnishment Release

A wage garnishment can severely impact your income. We act swiftly to address garnishments, negotiating with tax authorities to achieve a release or a more equitable payment arrangement.

Tax Preparation & Planning

Beyond resolution, we offer professional tax preparation and proactive planning services to help you maintain compliance and optimize your tax position for future years, preventing issues before they arise.
